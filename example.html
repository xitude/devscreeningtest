The Pink Gin & Tonic

Discover our modern twist on the quintessentially British Pink Gin.

Ingredients

Aromatic Tonic Water

50ml of juniper-rich gin - we recommend Plymouth Gin, Sipsmith VJOP, or a navy strength such as Bathtub.

Zest of lemon

Fresh ice

Method

Fill a highball glass with plenty of ice. Pour in your measure of gin.

Add Fever-Tree Aromatic Tonic from a fresh bottle.

Garnish with a generous zest of lemon.
