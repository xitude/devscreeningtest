The Pink Gin & Tonic

Discover our modern twist on the quintessentially British Pink Gin.

Ingredients

Aromatic Tonic Water

  • 50ml of juniper-rich gin - we recommend Plymouth Gin, Sipsmith VJOP, or a navy strength such as Bathtub.
  • Zest of lemon
  • Fresh ice

Method

  1. Fill a highball glass with plenty of ice. Pour in your measure of gin.
  2. Add Fever-Tree Aromatic Tonic from a fresh bottle.
  3. Garnish with a generous zest of lemon.
